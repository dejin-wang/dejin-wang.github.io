I am a genuine **badminton holic**. Outside my academic and research life, badminton has become one of the most important parts of my routine. I regularly play singles and doubles, join local clubs, and practice whenever I find a free court—whether on campus, in the community, or during travel.

The sport gives me a unique balance of speed, strategy, and focus. It keeps me active, clears my mind after long coding or research sessions, and brings a sense of rhythm and competitiveness into my daily life. The crisp sound of a clean smash or a well-placed drop shot is its own reward.

Although this page uses the same template as my technical talks, it represents a different side of me—one driven by passion, movement, and the joy of continuous improvement on and off the court.
