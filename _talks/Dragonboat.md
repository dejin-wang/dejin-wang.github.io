I am also deeply passionate about **dragon boat racing**, a sport that combines teamwork, rhythm, explosive power, and endurance. Dragon boat has become an important part of my personal life outside research. The intense coordination required—paddlers syncing perfectly with the drum beat—creates an atmosphere unlike any other sport.

I first joined a dragon boat team to try something new, and it quickly grew into a long-term commitment. Training sessions are challenging and physically demanding, but also incredibly rewarding. The feeling of an entire boat accelerating in perfect unison is one of the most satisfying experiences I’ve ever had in sports.
